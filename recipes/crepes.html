Softest Crepes!

Fully homemade crepes, an easy recipe.

This recipes utulises everyday home ingredients, you have in your kitchen right now. The crepes turn out soft with the most delicios crispy edge. They are good to combine with both sweet or salty foods.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 large cup of Flour
  • 1.5 large cups of Milk
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Break two eggs in a bowl and add milk
  2. Mix them till they are fully blended
  3. Add flour and salt and mix again till fully blended. The final consistency resembles a smoothie.
  4. Rub oil on a pan and heat it up properly.
  5. Pour the liquid on the pan in the middle and twist the pan such that it spreads all throughout.
  6. Once edges are starting to crisp, flip the crepe
  7. Once edges are brown, remove the crepe
  8. Repeat!
