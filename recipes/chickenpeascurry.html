Chicken, Peas, and Curry

Delicious Indian Receipe

This recipe combines coconut milk and tomatoes with peas and chicken to bring out a flavor combination that you will always be willing to eat!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Chick peas
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Curry Seasoning
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cut up onions into small pieces
  2. Put oil on a pan and add onions on low heat!
  3. Once onions are brown, increase heat and add diced tomatoes. Let cook till diced tomatoes are soft.
  4. Cut up chicken and cook it on a pan seperately
  5. Add chickpeas to tomatoes
  6. Put rice to boil in water
  7. Add curry seasoning and salt to the tomatoes
  8. Mix everything together

VOILA!
