Pastichio

A combination of Italian and Moroccan Ingenuity: FRIES AND CHEESE DISH

This recipes combines beschamel sauce, crispy fries, melted cheese and an addition of your choice to create a filling dish. This dish is found in Moroccan restaurant Cosa Mia and is available for $3.50 dollars.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Fries
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Melt 2 tbsp of butter at low medium hear on a pan
  2. Add flour 2 tbsp and stir untill none of the butter remain
  3. While on high heat add milk, stir, add cheese then stir, to get a final smoothie like consistency
  4. Preheat the oven
  5. Boil Oil and then add fries
  6. In a nice deep plate, put the fried fries
  7. Pour the sauce and extra cheese over the fries
  8. Put in oven for 3 min on 300 Fahrenheit

VOILA!
